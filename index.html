OpenMockup

I'm creating an standard for interchanging mockup files across systems.

I'm seeking to first create a community of people that use or are seeking mockup software, then do this together.

Non profit

I'm not seeking to develop a mockup software. There are dozens and some of them very good.

This is a non-profit project to bring the world an homogeneization. My small contribution to the society.
